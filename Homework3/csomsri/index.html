Dashboard Interactions

  • Selection: Click a bar in the type chart to filter the scatterplot, Pokémon dropdown, and stat profile by that selected type. Click the same bar again resets the type filter.
  • Brushing: Click and drag (highlight) over the scatterplot to select a group of Pokémon. Points inside the brushed region stay highlighted, while points outside the region fade.
  • Dropdown filtering: You can also use the Generation and Type dropdown menus from the Star Plot section. This allows you to filter the scatterplot and Pokémon dropdown by generation and type, giving you more control over which Pokémon to explore in the detail view.
  • Detail: You can either selected a Pokémon from the dropdown or highlight a scatterplot point to update the star plot with that Pokémon’s base stat profile.
  • Animated andtransitions: Bars grow when the type chart loads, scatterplot points fade during filtering, and star plot fades in when selected new.
