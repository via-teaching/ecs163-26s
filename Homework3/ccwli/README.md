# Student Performance Dashboard

This dashboard explores relationships between study habits, alcohol consumption, and student performance.

Views:
- Heatmap: overview of alcohol consumption patterns and average grades
- Scatterplot: relationship between study time and final grade
- Parallel Coordinates Plot: multivariate comparison of academic factors

Interactions:
- Heatmap selection filters related students
- PCP brushing filters subsets of students
- Scatterplot selection highlights individual students

Transitions:
Animated opacity and size transitions are used during filtering to help users track changes across linked views.

Design:
The dashboard follows a focus+context design paradigm.
The heatmap provides an overview, while the scatterplot and PCP support detailed exploration of subsets and individuals.
