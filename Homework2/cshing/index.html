Student Alcohol Consumption

Alcohol, Lifestyle, and Academic Performance

This dashboard explores how weekday and weekend drinking patterns connect with final grades, study habits, social activity, and absences across the Math and Portuguese student datasets. Use the scatter plot to compare alcohol use and grades, the grouped bar chart to compare study-time and alcohol groups, and the parallel coordinates chart to follow one student profile across several variables.
