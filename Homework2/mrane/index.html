University Student Mental Health Dashboard

This dashboard explores student mental health in terms of its correlation to gender, year of study, GPA, and condition type. For an overview of the population and relevant variables, observe the Sankey diagram on the left, checking ribbon size to see how students flow from gender and year variables into condition and treatment status. Then, examine whether GPA ranges predict condition rates using the bar chart on the top right, and finally compare how Depression, Anxiety, and Panic Attacks fluctuate for students in every year using the heatmap on the bottom right.
